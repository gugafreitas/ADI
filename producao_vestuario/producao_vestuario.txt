Independent Variables
1.	rowID: register id
2.	date: Date in DD/MM/YYYY
3.	department: Associated department with the instance
4.	team: Associated team number with the instance
5.	targeted_productivity: Targeted productivity set by the Authority for each team for each day
6.	smv: Standard Minute Value, it is the allocated time for a task
7.	wip: Work in progress. Includes the number of unfinished items for products
8.	over_time: Represents the amount of overtime by each team in minutes
9.	incentive: Represents the amount of financial incentive (in BDT) that enables or motivates a particular course of action
10.	idle_time: The amount of time when the production was interrupted due to several reasons
11.	idle_men: The number of workers who were idle due to production interruption
12.	no_of_workers: Number of workers in each team
13.	no_of_style_change: Number of changes in the style of a particular product

Dependent Variables
14.	actual_productivity: The actual % of productivity that was delivered by the workers
